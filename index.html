The discrepancy between the traditional yoga approach and the modern lifestyle.

Human body is made for continuous activity. As our lifestyle coerced us to sit more and move less, the traditional yoga postures became rather inapproachable. We at Yogarambha took to a rather simple approach to the problem. With intelligent amalgamation of modern movement techniques with ancient yogic principles, our method is an attempt to bridge this rapidly widening gap.

PHILOSOPHY

From office chair to Yoga mat

Over the years we have experimented with our approach, using our own bodies as petri dish to reframe and polish this technique to bring you a unique style which can be safely practiced and modified to suit what our students have aptly started calling ‘the chair body’.
